NVA Project

A Non-Value Added (NVA) project focuses on identifying and eliminating activities that do not add value to the customer or the final product. These activities consume resources and time without contributing to the overall quality or functionality of the product or service. The goal of an NVA project is to streamline processes, reduce waste, and improve efficiency
